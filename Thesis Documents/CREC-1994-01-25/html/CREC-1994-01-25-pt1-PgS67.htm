              ADJOURNMENT OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

  Mr. KERRY. I ask unanimous consent that the Senate proceed to the 
immediate consideration of House Concurrent Resolution 198 relating to 
the adjournment of the House of Representatives just received from the 
House, that the concurrent resolution be agreed to, and the motion to 
reconsider be laid upon the table.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
  The concurrent resolution (H. Con. Res. 198) was agreed to.
