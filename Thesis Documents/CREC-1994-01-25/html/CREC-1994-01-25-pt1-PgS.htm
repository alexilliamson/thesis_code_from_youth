Vol. 140


WASHINGTON, TUESDAY, JANUARY 25, 1994

No. 1


Senate

  The 25th day of January being the day prescribed by House Joint 
Resolution 190 for the meeting of the 2d session of the 103d Congress, 
the Senate assembled in its Chamber at the Capitol at 12 noon.
  The Senate was called to order by the President pro tempore [Mr. 
Byrd].
  The PRESIDENT pro tempore. The Senate will come to order.
  The prayer will be led by the Senate Chaplain, the Reverend Dr. 
Richard C. Halverson.
  Doctor Halverson, please.
