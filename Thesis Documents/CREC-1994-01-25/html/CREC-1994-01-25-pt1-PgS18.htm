                                 RECESS

  The PRESIDENT pro tempore. Under the order previously entered, the 
Senate will now stand in recess until the hour of 2:30 p.m. today.
  Thereupon, at 1:20 p.m., the Senate recessed until 2:30 p.m.; 
whereupon, the Senate reassembled when called to order by the Presiding 
Officer (Mr. Kohl).
