  PERMISSION TO ENTERTAIN MOTION TO SUSPEND THE RULES ON TOMORROW FOR 
                 CONSIDERATION OF HOUSE RESOLUTION 323

  Mr. GEPHARDT. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent that it be in 
order tomorrow, Wednesday, January 26, 1994, for the Speaker to 
entertain a motion to suspend the rules and adopt House Resolution 323, 
relating to the treatment of Hugo Princz, a United States citizen, by 
the Federal Republic of Germany.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Is there objection to the request of the 
gentleman from Missouri?
  There was no objection.
