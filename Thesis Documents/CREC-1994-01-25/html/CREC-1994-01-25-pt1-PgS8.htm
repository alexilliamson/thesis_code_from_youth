            VOTE ON MOTION TO INSTRUCT THE SERGEANT AT ARMS

  The PRESIDENT pro tempore. Under the order, the Senate will now 
proceed to vote on the motion to instruct the Sergeant at Arms to 
request the attendance of absent Senators. A rollcall vote has not been 
ordered.
  The majority leader.
  Mr. MITCHELL. Mr. President, I ask for the yeas and nays.
  The PRESIDENT pro tempore. The yeas and nays have been requested. Is 
the demand sustained?
  It is.
  The yeas and nays were ordered.
  The PRESIDENT pro tempore. The clerk will call the roll.
  The legislative clerk called the roll.
  The result was announced--yeas 100, nays 0, as follows:
