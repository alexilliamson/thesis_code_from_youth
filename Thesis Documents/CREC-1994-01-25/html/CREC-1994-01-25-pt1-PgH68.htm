                        JOINT SESSION DISSOLVED

  The SPEAKER. The Chair declares the joint session of the two Houses 
now dissolved.
  Accordingly, at 10 o'clock p.m. and 23 minutes, the joint session of 
the two Houses was dissolved. The Members of the Senate retired to 
their Chamber.
